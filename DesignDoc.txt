==== DESIGN DOCUMENT ===

== Introduction
    This will be a Java Swing-based Spending calculator that uses button presses to add text field inputs into a JTable
    of expenses and incomes. A running total of the account will be stored in the far right column of the JTable, and
    the entries will take the form of debits and credits.

== GUI Design
    The center of the screen stretching from the top will be the JTable. South of that will be the input suite where the
    buttons and text fields reside.

== Model Design
    The model will be made up of AccountingItems, which will have an amount, description, and date. There will be an
    account object, which will load a list of these accounting items that can be loaded and saved.

== External Connections
    Will be able to load .csv files into a full account with accounting items into the JTable.

== Extensions
    Can add summary information using a JMenubar at the top, as well as functionality to switch to a new account at
    runtime.
    Can add the ability to edit certain accounting items and members of the account.
